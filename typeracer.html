Introduction

During the summer of 2018 I decided to train myself on "proper" touch typing. I was already familiar with typeracer at the time, and as soon as I found out I could download my data I knew I wanted to make a project out of it.

This scatterplot depicts a high-level summary of my progress thus far. The following sections will explore my typeracer activity, individual race analysis, and a deep dive on my WPM improvement.

Activity

These charts illustrate my racing activity broken down by year, month, day of the week, and hour (in local time). They largely speak for themselves, but there are a few points worth discussing.
